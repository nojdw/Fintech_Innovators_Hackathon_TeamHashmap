Mango Fi is a personal finance terminal built for Singapore-based investors, delivering a unified view of total financial health across traditional and digital assets.
What it does:
The dashboard consolidates brokerage holdings (via yfinance), mock bank accounts (DBS/Standard Chartered), CPF balances, private assets, and digital/crypto wallets into a single authenticated interface, directly addressing the fragmentation problem of modern multi-asset portfolios.
Key features:
1. Portfolio tab: live-priced positions with per-currency display, P&L tracking, and transaction history
2. Banking tab: 12-month cash flow analysis across multiple accounts with timeline filtering
3. Analysis tab: a proprietary Financial Health Score (diversification, risk, income, valuation, quality, liquidity), concentration warnings, benchmark comparisons, and AI-powered composition analysis via Gemini
4. Retirement tab — Singapore-specific CPF-aware retirement projections with SWR corpus modelling, inflation adjustment, and dividend income goal tracking

How it solves the problem:
Rather than disconnected investments, banking, and retirement planning across separate platforms, Mango Fi aggregates all asset classes into one read-only, secure terminal. The Health Score and AI insights then surface actionable signals, and not just data, giving the investor a genuine wellness assessment of their complete financial picture.
